Features

The most popular system for business automation enthusiasts focused on privacy. See why.

Reduce false positives with local object detection

Traditional systems can require hours of fine-tuning to reduce false positive rates because they rely on simple motion detection. However, through our advanced detection technology, even modest hardware can perform analysis to determine if the movement is truly a person, car, or other object of interest. With Rubi Vision's local processing, there’s no need to pay for your camera footage to be sent to the cloud for analysis.

Stop reviewing shadows and wind and start reviewing detections that matter

Let Rubi Vision's AI deliver your video feeds for you. With a machine running on a mid-range processor, our AI can perform over 1000 object detections per second, ensuring not a single frame is missed.

Fine tune your events and alerts with zones

Rubi Vision tracks objects in real-time and can pinpoint the exact moment a person starts walking up your steps or when a car enters your driveway. Receive your notifications via email based on precise information.

Universal AI Integration with Rubi Vision

Give your business eyes by integrating our advanced recognition with any filming device. Rubi Vision seamlessly integrates with all cameras not locked into their own proprietary platforms. Additionally, it supports local USB cameras, microphones, files, and desktop recording.
